What is StudentVC?

StudentVC is a revolutionary digital credential platform that transforms how academic achievements are issued, stored, and verified. Built on cutting-edge cryptographic technology, it puts students in complete control of their educational records.

For Students

  • Own and control your academic credentials digitally
  • Share only the information required for verification
  • Instant verification without contacting your university
  • Works with any compatible digital wallet app

For Organizations

  • Instantly verify academic credentials without delays
  • Eliminate credential fraud with cryptographic proof
  • Request only the specific information you need
  • Integrate with existing verification systems

How StudentVC Works

StudentVC uses advanced cryptographic techniques to ensure credentials are authentic, tamper-proof, and privacy-preserving.

1

Issue

Universities issue digital credentials using BBS+ signatures, which enable selective disclosure and privacy protection.

2

Store

Students receive credentials in their mobile wallet, maintaining complete control over their academic records.

3

Verify

Organizations can instantly verify credentials while students control exactly what information is shared.
